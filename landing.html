What it does

Everything a requirement needs, in one place

Guided discovery on the left, a living document on the right. ReqPub keeps the structure rigorous so the team can focus on the thinking.

Structured discovery

A single guided worksheet across 16 sections: overview, users, scope, metrics, requirements, data and privacy, and more.

Testable by default

Every requirement carries a permanent ID, a measurable fit criterion, and a MoSCoW priority. No requirement is "done" without acceptance.

Versioned baselines

Generate v1.0 and beyond. See exactly what changed each version, down to which requirements were added, modified, or removed.

AI evaluation criteria

For probabilistic features, capture golden dataset metrics, hallucination guardrails, and numeric thresholds, so verification goes beyond a single expected output.

Curated share links

Send a plain language PRD review link, or an app testing link for pilots. Reviewers never receive internal requirement detail; it stays excluded at the source.

Feedback & discovery

Triage pilot feedback, log discovery conversations, and promote decisions straight into traceable requirements.

How it works

From a blank page to a baseline in three moves

1

Answer the worksheet

Work through the guided questions. The document on the right builds itself as you type, section by section.

2

Generate a version

Baseline v1.0 with one click. Each new version records the author and a precise summary of what changed.

3

Share & collect feedback

Publish a frozen review link or pilot link. Reviews and bug reports flow back into the project, organized by version.

Export

Take the document anywhere

A single source of truth that leaves cleanly in whatever format the next reader expects.
